Dhoom: 3 makes Rs 500 cr. That's right, 500 cr

Written by Gitanjali Roy | January 07, 2014 08:50 IST (New Delhi)
inShare0
Print 
Dhoom: 3 released on December 20, 2013.
2014 is already a blockbuster year for Bollywood. Dhoom: 3 has earned Rs 500 crores and Aamir Khan is on top once again. In 2013, Shah Rukh Khan's Chennai Express and Hrithik Roshan's Krrish 3 briefly shattered 3 Idiots' three year run at the top of the blockbuster lists. With Dhoom: 3, Aamir reclaims his most valuable player position.

Since it released on December 20, 2013, Dhoom: 3 has collected Rs 501.35 crores worldwide, making Rs 351.29 in India and Rs 150.06 abroad.

Trade analyst Taran Adarsh tweeted: "In terms of *gross BO numbers*, #Dhoom3 is the first Hindi film to cross Rs 500 cr mark *worldwide*. Creates History."

Producers Yash Raj Films said in a statement: "Dhoom: 3 continues to create history at both the Indian and overseas box office. It is now officially the first Indian film to cross Rs 500 crore worldwide."

Dhoom: 3, co-starring Katrina Kaif, Abhishek Bachchan and Uday Chopra, is one-third of the hit series, previous installments of which starred Hrithik Roshan, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, John Abraham and Bipasha Basu.
